# Project Output Visualizations

This directory contains the high-resolution, professional charts and plots generated during the Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) and Model Training stages.

## Visualizations Present

### 1. Demographic & Financial Distributions

- **[histogram.png](file:///c:/Users/laksh/CreditCard/11_Outputs/histogram.png):** Displays the distribution of annual income across applicants, revealing a right-skewed distribution.
- **[countplot.png](file:///c:/Users/laksh/CreditCard/11_Outputs/countplot.png):** Summarizes the count of applicants across different occupation types, with laborers being the most common.
- **[income_type_pie.png](file:///c:/Users/laksh/CreditCard/11_Outputs/income_type_pie.png):** A pie chart displaying the proportion of income stream categories.
- **[boxplot.png](file:///c:/Users/laksh/CreditCard/11_Outputs/boxplot.png):** Shows income distribution across education levels, revealing that higher education levels are associated with higher median incomes.
- **[age_vs_employment.png](file:///c:/Users/laksh/CreditCard/11_Outputs/age_vs_employment.png):** A scatter plot showing the relationship between age and employment duration, categorized by income type.
- **[pairplot.png](file:///c:/Users/laksh/CreditCard/11_Outputs/pairplot.png):** A grid plot displaying pairwise relationships and distributions for numerical features.
- **[target_distribution.png](file:///c:/Users/laksh/CreditCard/11_Outputs/target_distribution.png):** Shows the class distribution of the target variable ($y$), representing the ratio of approved to rejected applicants.
